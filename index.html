A Magic Eye picture hides a depth map inside a repeating pattern. Points that sit closer to you are drawn slightly closer together. Measure that spacing everywhere and the hidden surface falls out — no eye-crossing required.

Drop a stereogram image here, or paste one with ⌘V / Ctrl+V.
